💡 Cyber Safety Tips

Follow these important practices to protect yourself online.

🔒 Use Strong Passwords

Always create complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information.

📧 Beware of Suspicious Emails

Never click links or download attachments from unknown senders. Always verify the sender’s address carefully.

🔐 Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Turn on 2FA for your accounts to add an extra layer of security against hackers.

🌐 Check URLs Carefully

Before entering sensitive information, ensure the site starts with https:// and is the correct domain.

📱 Keep Software Updated

Always update your apps, browsers, and operating system to protect against new threats.

🧠 Stay Aware & Educated

Regularly learn about phishing trends and scams to stay alert and secure.
